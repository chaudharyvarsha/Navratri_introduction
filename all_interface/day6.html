Day 6: Katyayani

About Goddess Katyayani

Katyayani is the sixth form of Goddess Durga, worshipped on the sixth day of Navratri. She was born to Sage Katyayan after his penance, hence called Katyayani. She is a fierce warrior goddess who destroys evil forces.

She is depicted with four hands, carrying a sword and lotus, while two hands bless devotees. Riding a lion, she represents courage, righteousness, and victory over demons. She is associated with the Ajna (third eye) chakra, symbolizing wisdom and spiritual awakening.
