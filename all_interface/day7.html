Day 7: Kalaratri

About Goddess Kalaratri

Kalaratri is the seventh form of Goddess Durga, worshipped on the seventh day of Navratri. She is the fiercest form, with a dark complexion, flowing hair, and a fearless posture. She rides a donkey and has three eyes glowing like fire.

She has four hands, carrying a sword and iron hook, while the other two are in mudras of blessing and protection. Despite her terrifying appearance, she blesses devotees with courage and removes fear and negativity. She governs the Sahasrara (crown) chakra, symbolizing liberation and knowledge of the ultimate truth.
